What it is

Where your system becomes a thinking partner

Most AI setups are a wrapper around a remote API — fast when the network is up, broken when it's not, and always logging to someone else's servers. EVAA runs on the device. The model runs on the device. The memory lives on the device. The only thing that changes is who controls it.

EVAA Cognition provides structured reasoning, context fusion, memory retrieval, and tool orchestration as a resident engine — not a service you call. It's always present, always local, and governed from the ground up by the AACP protocol layer so its behavior is transparent and predictable.
